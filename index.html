Features

Everything you need to stay secure

PassGuard Pro goes beyond simple strength meters — it is a complete password intelligence suite built with real security standards in mind.

Real-Time Analyzer
Evaluates passwords against 8 security rules with entropy scoring, pattern detection, live strength history chart, and visual character breakdown — all as you type.
Core
Live Breach Detection
Checks against HaveIBeenPwned's database of 10+ billion compromised passwords via k-anonymity — your actual password is never transmitted anywhere.
Security
Password Generator
Four modes: Random characters, memorable Passphrases, Pronounceable passwords, and PIN codes — with full toggles, sliders, and live strength preview.
Generator
Crack Time Estimator
Calculates exactly how long your password would survive a brute-force attack at 10 billion guesses per second — from "Instant" to "Millions of years".
Analysis
Password Vault
Save passwords with custom labels, masked display with show/hide, strength scores, crack time estimates, and one-click CSV export for all entries.
Storage
Health Score Dashboard
An overall 0–100 security ring score calculated from all your vault passwords, with personalized recommendations and a full session activity log.
Dashboard
Security Quiz
A timed 5-question cybersecurity quiz with a 30-second countdown per question, instant feedback, detailed explanations, and a final score card.
Education
Security Tips Library
10 rotating expert tips covering password hygiene, 2FA, phishing awareness, and secure browsing. Auto-rotates every 8 seconds and pauses on hover.
Education
Auth Gate & Accounts
Users must register or sign in before accessing any feature. Session-persisted accounts with a personal dashboard showing your strength score and vault stats.
Auth
How It Works

Three steps to password safety

1
Create Your Account
Register with your name, email, and password. Your credentials are stored only in your browser session — nothing is ever sent to a server.
2
Analyze & Generate
Get real-time feedback on strength, entropy, crack time, breach status, and improvement tips. Generate stronger replacements in one click.
3
Track Your Security
Save passwords to your vault, monitor your overall health score, take the security quiz, and export your vault as a CSV for safekeeping.
